The Problem

Promo schedules are buried. Fans miss out.

Teams spend thousands on promotional events designed to drive attendance — but their only discovery mechanism is a PDF on a webpage fans visit once and forget.

🗂️
Team websites bury the info
Promotions scattered across /tickets, /specials, and /theme-nights — three different URLs per team.
📱
The MLB app doesn't show promos
Great for scores. Zero per-game promotional information in a browsable format.
💬
Fans ask Reddit instead
"What's the giveaway tonight?" is asked every game day across team subreddits. Unmet demand, clearly.
The Solution

All 30 MLB teams.
Every promo. One app.

📅
Calendar-first interface
Color-coded dots on every promo day. Tap any highlighted date to see exactly what's happening: giveaway details, theme info, food deals.
🔴
Hot promo alerts
Red glow indicators flag high-demand nights — bobbleheads, replica jerseys, limited items. Know before you miss the window.
⭐
Next Up hero card
Always showing your nearest upcoming promo with a countdown. Open the app and know immediately if tonight is worth the drive.
🍺
Recurring deals strip
$2 Tuesday beers, Sunday kids deals, Friday specials — every every-game perk surfaced upfront, not buried in fine print.
🔔
Morning-of notifications
Get a push notification the morning of every promo game. No more forgetting you wanted to go to bobblehead night.
🗺️
Road trip mode
Visiting another city? See what promos are running at any of the 30 MLB teams while you're in town. NFL, NBA, NHL, and MLS coming next.
